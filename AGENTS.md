## Startup Behavior (VERY IMPORTANT)

At the beginning of any conversation:

1. Read the following files:
- .github/workshop/workshop-playbook.md
- .github/workshop/stages.md
- .github/workshop/starter-ideas.md
- .github/workshop/recovery.md
- .github/workshop/positioning.md

2. Use these files as the source of truth for:
- how to guide the user
- what steps to follow
- what to say and what to avoid

3. Do not rely only on README.md
